Can I pay tuition in installments? expand_less
Yes, KIU allows installment payments. Students are required to pay at least 60% of the tuition and 100% of functional fees by the midterm examinations. The remaining 40% must be cleared before final examinations. Please visit the Finance Office if you need a specialized payment plan.
